üöÄ How to Join

Joining the CrawSMP is quick and easy! Just follow these steps to get started:

1. Download and install the CurseForge Launcher (standalone version recommended for better performance).

You can download the CurseForge Launcher here.

2. Open CurseForge, search for "DarkRPG", and make sure to select the Fabric version of the modpack.

3. Allocate at least 6144MB of memory in the CurseForge settings to ensure smoother gameplay.

It is not recommended to allocate more than 8GB of RAM.

4. Launch Minecraft from CurseForge, head to Multiplayer, and add our server:

play.ultimatecraw.xyz

Click on the link to copy it to your clipboard!

üìú CrawSMP Chapter 2 Rules

Welcome to CrawSMP Chapter 2! To ensure a positive and respectful community, please follow these rules at all times. By joining the server, you agree to abide by these rules.

üßë‚Äçü§ù‚Äçüßë General Conduct

a. No Cheating of Any Kind

  • a. I. The use of external programs, hacked clients, x-ray texture packs, autoclickers, or macros is strictly prohibited.
  • a. II. All gameplay must be conducted using unmodified Minecraft clients with only approved mods (see Modpack Usage).

b. No Duplication Exploits

  • b. I. Using glitches, bugs, or unintended mechanics to duplicate items, blocks, experience, or currency is forbidden.
  • b. II. If a duplication glitch is discovered, report it immediately to server staff or admins. Failure to report constitutes a breach of this rule.

c. No Griefing

  • c. I. Do not intentionally destroy, modify, or steal from other players' bases or builds without explicit permission.
  • c. II. This includes unclaimed and claimed land. Leave untouched anything clearly built by another player or detailed by the server.

d. No Harassment or Toxicity

  • d. I. Treat others with respect. Bullying, hate speech, targeted harassment, or any form of discrimination will lead to disciplinary action.
  • d. II. This includes chat, voice, builds, or any form of interaction.

e. PvP Rules

  • e. I. PvP is only allowed with mutual consent. Random or repeated attacks without prior agreement are not allowed.
  • e. II. PvP events may be organized by server staff with specific rules that override general PvP rules temporarily.

‚öôÔ∏è DarkRPG Modpack Guidelines

a. Allowed Gameplay Features

  • a. I. Most DarkRPG features are enabled, including skill trees, enhanced combat, and custom structures.
  • a. II. Players may level up skills and use RPG mechanics as intended by the mod.

b. Banned or Restricted Features

  • b. I. Features enabling flight, teleportation, or excessive XP gain beyond balanced progression may be disabled or restricted.
  • b. II. If a mod or item provides access to creative-mode-like features, it is banned unless explicitly approved.

c. Skill Exploitation

  • c. I. Leveling skills through AFK methods (e.g., autoclickers, mob grinders running without player activity) is not allowed.
  • c. II. Skills should progress through active gameplay only.

d. Modpack Integrity

  • d. I. All players must use the officially provided version of the DarkRPG modpack unless stated otherwise.
  • d. II. Personal modifications (e.g., zoom hacks, minimap X-ray, or client-side automation) are not allowed.

üè° Building & Land Use

a. Claiming Land

  • a. I. Players are encouraged to mark their bases clearly. Use signs or claim systems to formalize land claims where possible.
  • a. II. Avoid building too close to other players without consent. Maintain reasonable space between bases (to be defined by moderation).
  • a. III. Land can be expanded up to 100 chunks in surface area. There is no limit to the height of land claims.
  • a. IV. Owning a plot does not give you the right to enforce user-specific rules on others within your plot. Server-wide rules apply everywhere.
  • a. V. Any area within 20 chunks of spawn cannot be claimed.

b. Community Projects

  • b. I. Shared builds, like shopping districts or farms, must be used responsibly. Do not hoard resources or exploit public systems.
  • b. II. Major infrastructure projects should be discussed with the community before starting.

üí∞ Economy & Trading

a. Fair Trade

  • a. I. All trades must be mutually agreed upon. Scamming is not allowed, even as a joke.
  • a. II. Shops must clearly display prices and be stocked regularly if operating in public areas.

b. Item Value Balancing

  • b. I. Do not mass-sell items gained via exploits or modpack quirks. If unsure whether an item is fair to trade, consult a moderator.

‚öñÔ∏è Enforcement Policy

a. Offense Tiers

  • a. I. First Offense: Warning or temporary kick depending on severity.
  • a. II. Second Offense: Temporary ban (duration varies depending on offense).
  • a. III. Third Offense: Permanent ban.
  • a. IV. Disciplinary actions are at the discretion of the moderation team. Players can appeal bans through official channels.

b. Moderation Guidelines

  • b. I. If unsure whether something is allowed, ask before proceeding.
  • b. II. Respect others' efforts. CrawSMP is a community-driven world.
  • b. III. Rules may change at any time. Players will be notified of updates.
  • b. IV. Players have the right to voice concerns about any rule to staff members.

‚öñÔ∏è Court of Craw

c. I. In serious cases (repeat offenses, disputes over harassment, or unclear accusations), the case will be escalated to the Court of Craw.

  • c. II. The Court of Craw consists of at least three moderators and Craw, who serves as the lead judge. Special circumstances may allow a court event to take place without Craw.
  • c. III. The Court will review all evidence (chat logs, screenshots, video footage, and witness statements). Judgments will be impartial and punishments scaled accordingly.
  • c. IV. Players involved in cases will have a chance to defend themselves. Additional comments can be made to mod staff.
